About Nesarahippla

An indie studio making games that feel like India.

Our Story

Nesarahippla started with a simple question: why don't mobile games reflect the beauty of Indian culture? While the world's biggest puzzle games use generic candy and jewels, we saw an opportunity to create something deeply personal — games built around Rangoli patterns, Diya lamps, Lotus flowers, and the vibrant colours of our festivals.

We're a small indie studio based in India, driven by a passion for combining traditional art with modern game design. Our first title, Rangoli Rush, is a match-3 puzzle game where every level creates a beautiful Rangoli pattern — turning gameplay into art.

We build games for the 500+ million mobile gamers in India. Games that run on budget phones, speak regional languages, and respect your wallet with fair pricing starting at ₹10.

What We Believe

Culture First

India's artistic traditions — Rangoli, Kolam, Pookalam — are thousands of years old. We bring these into the digital age, not as decoration, but as core gameplay.

Accessible to All

A game isn't truly for India if it only works on flagship phones. We optimise for 2GB RAM devices, small download sizes, and offline play.

Fair Monetization

No pay-to-win. No predatory mechanics. Our in-app purchases enhance the experience — they never gate it. You can enjoy every level for free.

Your Language

We launch in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and English simultaneously. Language should never be a barrier to fun.
