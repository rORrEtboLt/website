Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstalled Rangoli Rush or switched devices, your purchases are tied to your Google Play account. Make sure you're signed into the same Google account, then open the game — your purchases should restore automatically. If they don't, contact us with your Google Play order ID.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled through Google Play. Visit your Google Play order history, find the purchase, and tap "Request a refund." If Google denies the refund and you believe it was made in error (e.g., by a child), contact us and we'll do our best to help.

The game is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps: make sure you have the latest version from Google Play, restart your device, clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > Rangoli Rush > Clear Cache). If the issue persists, email us with your device model and Android version.

How do I save my progress?

Your game progress is saved automatically on your device. To back it up across devices, sign into Google Play Games when prompted. Your progress, scores, and achievements will sync.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Rangoli Rush's core gameplay works fully offline. You only need an internet connection for in-app purchases, cloud saves, leaderboards, and downloading new festival events.

How do I delete my data?

Email privacy@nesarahippla.com with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will delete your analytics and support data within 30 days. To delete local game data, simply uninstall the app.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support requests within 48 hours. Privacy and data requests are handled within 30 days as required by applicable regulations.
