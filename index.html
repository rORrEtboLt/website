About

Art rooted in culture

I'm an independent game artist drawing from India's rich visual traditions — Rangoli, Kolam, Madhubani, and the living art of its festivals. Every pixel is a deliberate choice rooted in that inheritance.

My games are built for the 500 million+ mobile players across India who deserve to see their world reflected back at them — in their language, their art, their celebrations.
