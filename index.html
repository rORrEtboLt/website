What We Do

Games that feel like home

We believe the best games reflect the players who play them. Our games are built with Indian art, festivals, and traditions at their core — not as an afterthought.

🎩

Culturally Rooted

Every game draws from India's rich artistic traditions — Rangoli, Kolam, Madhubani, and more. Regional festivals drive in-game events throughout the year.

📱

Built for India

Optimized for budget smartphones (2GB RAM), small download sizes under 50MB, and UPI-friendly price points starting at ₹10. Works in tier-2 and tier-3 cities.

🌈

Regional Languages

All our games launch in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and English from day one. Because everyone deserves to play in their language.

Our Values

How we build

Small studio, big ambitions. We care about craft, fairness, and the players who trust us with their time.

Fair Monetization

We never paywall progress. Every level is completable without spending. In-app purchases are for boosters, cosmetics, and convenience — never requirements.

Player Privacy

We collect the minimum data needed to run our games. We never sell personal data. Read our full privacy policy for complete transparency.

Continuous Content

New levels, festival events, and features every two weeks. We listen to player feedback and ship fast. Our games get better every month.
