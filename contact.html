Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Rangoli Rush → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Google account used for the original purchase.

Can I request a refund?

Refunds for Google Play purchases are handled through Google. Visit your Google Play order history to request a refund, or email us at support@nesarahippla.com.

How do I delete my account data?

Email privacy@nesarahippla.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email.

The game crashes on my device. What do I do?

Please email support@nesarahippla.com with your device model, Android version, and a description of when the crash occurs. Screenshots help!
