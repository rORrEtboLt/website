Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026 • Applies to: All Nesarahippla apps, games, and websites

In short: We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and you can contact us anytime to ask questions or request deletion. Individual games may have additional privacy details — see their specific privacy policies.

1. About Nesarahippla

Nesarahippla is an independent mobile games studio based in Chikmagalur, Karnataka, India. This privacy policy applies to all games, applications, and websites published by Nesarahippla, including but not limited to Rangoli Rush, nesarahippla.com, and any future titles.

2. What We Collect

Across our games and website, we may collect:

  • Device and technical data: Device model, OS version, screen size, language, and timezone. Used to ensure games work correctly on your device.
  • Usage analytics: How you interact with our games (levels played, session length, features used). Collected via Firebase Analytics with a random device identifier.
  • Crash data: Automatic crash reports to help us fix bugs. Collected via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Purchase data: Transaction records from Google Play Billing. We do not process or store your payment details.
  • Support communications: If you contact us, we store your email and message content.
  • Website analytics: Standard web analytics (pages viewed, referrer, browser type) for nesarahippla.com.

3. What We Don't Collect

  • Real names, phone numbers, physical addresses
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or file access
  • Precise GPS location
  • Biometric data
  • Social media account information

4. How We Use Data

  • Operating and improving our games
  • Fixing crashes and bugs
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Responding to support requests
  • Measuring marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Google (Firebase, Play Services): Analytics, crash reporting, purchase processing, and cloud saves.
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Data Retention

Analytics: up to 14 months. Crash reports: 90 days. Support emails: up to 2 years. Purchase records: as required by tax regulations. Game progress: stored locally on your device; cloud saves persist until you delete them.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

8. Children

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our games do not require account creation. If you believe a child has sent us personal information, contact us for immediate deletion.

9. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics via Android settings
  • Reset or delete your advertising ID
  • Uninstall any app to delete all local data

Contact nesara.hippla@gmail.com for any data requests. We respond within 30 days.

10. International Transfers

Analytics data may be processed by Google on servers outside India. Google maintains appropriate data protection standards.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through our apps or website. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
