Rangoli Rush — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026 • Effective: March 22, 2026

Summary: Rangoli Rush collects minimal data needed to operate the game. We never sell your personal information. We use analytics to improve the game and process purchases securely through Google Play Billing.

1. Who We Are

Rangoli Rush is developed and published by Nesarahippla, an independent game studio based in Chikmagalur, Karnataka, India. In this policy, "we," "us," and "our" refer to Nesarahippla.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@nesarahippla.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Support requests: If you email us for support, we collect your email address and the content of your message.
  • In-app purchases: Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a transaction ID and the product purchased, but we do not receive or store your payment card details, UPI ID, or bank information.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, and language settings. Used to optimize game performance across devices.
  • Game progress: Level completion, scores, and achievements. Stored locally on your device and optionally backed up via Google Play Games Services.
  • Analytics data: Session duration, levels played, feature usage, and crash reports. Collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the game. This data is associated with a randomly generated device identifier, not your personal identity.
  • Advertising identifier: Google Advertising ID, used for measuring the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. You can reset or opt out of this identifier in your Android device settings.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files.
  • We do not collect precise location data (GPS). We may infer general region from IP address for analytics purposes only.
  • We do not collect biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game operation: To save your progress, process purchases, and deliver in-game content.
  • Improvement: To understand which levels are too easy or too hard, which features players enjoy, and where the game crashes. This helps us release better updates.
  • Customer support: To respond to your support requests and resolve issues.
  • Marketing measurement: To understand how players discover our game and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We share data with the following service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics): For analytics and crash reporting. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Google handles all payment information.
  • Google Play Games Services: For optional cloud save of game progress, leaderboards, and achievements.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months in Firebase Analytics, after which it is automatically deleted or anonymized. Crash reports are retained for 90 days. Support correspondence is retained for up to 2 years. Purchase records are retained as required by tax and accounting regulations.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure server infrastructure provided by Google Cloud, and access controls limiting who can view analytics data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Rangoli Rush is rated for all ages ("Everyone"). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or login. If you believe a child has provided us personal information through a support request, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request what data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of analytics: You can opt out of Firebase Analytics by disabling "Usage & diagnostics" in your Android device settings.
  • Reset advertising ID: Go to Android Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID.
  • Uninstall: You can delete all local game data by uninstalling the app.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@nesarahippla.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Links

The game may contain links to external websites (e.g., our support page, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Our analytics data is processed by Google, which may transfer data to servers outside India. Google complies with applicable data protection frameworks. By using Rangoli Rush, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice within the game or on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance.
