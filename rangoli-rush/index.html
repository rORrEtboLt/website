Game Features

Why players love Rangoli Rush

A puzzle game that celebrates India's artistic heritage. Every match brings you closer to completing a beautiful Rangoli.

🎨

5 Unique Gem Types

Match Diyas, Lotuses, Peacocks, Bells, and Rangoli diamonds — each with hand-crafted visual designs inspired by traditional Indian art.

🌈

500+ Levels

Journey across 25 Indian cities from Jaipur to Kochi. Each city features unique Rangoli traditions and progressively challenging puzzles.

🎆

Festival Events

Special themed levels during Diwali, Holi, Pongal, Navratri, Onam, and more. Exclusive limited-time Rangoli designs you can share.

💥

Combos & Boosters

Chain cascading matches for massive combos. Use Hammers, Color Bombs, and Row Blasters to clear tough levels.

🖼

Rangoli Collection

Every completed level fills in a beautiful Rangoli pattern. Collect and share your completed Rangolis on WhatsApp.

👤

Play in Your Language

Available in Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and Marathi. Settings, tutorials, and UI fully localized.

How to Play

Simple to learn, impossible to put down

☝

1. Tap & Swap

Tap a gem, then tap an adjacent gem to swap them.

✨

2. Match 3+

Line up 3 or more identical gems in a row or column to pop them.

🎨

3. Create Rangoli

Score enough points to fill in the Rangoli pattern and complete the level.
